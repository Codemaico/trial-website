Watch all of the 2022-2023 premier league fixtures after it's return from the world cup.

The 2022-2023 premier league will restart after the world cup break on December, 26. The current leaders on the table Arsenal will start the second campaign against West Ham at the Emirate's stadium. While last season's champions and currently second at the table Manchester city will face Leeds United at the Elland Road.

Manchester City will once again be the club to beat in the Premier League. The Sky Blues' already star-studded roster added another game-changing player in Erling Haaland, who was acquired in a blockbuster transfer from Borussia Dortmund.
